✅ Bottom line:

SharePoint is a goldmine for PS Forge because so many site and permission tasks repeat.

You can offer simple wizards to generate PnP or On-Prem cmdlets with correct syntax and optional safety controls.

Reporting templates (permissions, site inventory, guest access, external sharing) are especially valuable.
